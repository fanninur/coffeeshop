# Tudey Coffee

## Project Overview

Tudey Coffee is a modern coffee shop designed for young people who seek more than just a cup of coffee. It is a place to relax, work remotely, meet friends, and enjoy a cozy atmosphere with quality coffee and food.

The website should reflect the café's identity as a lifestyle destination rather than simply a place to buy coffee.

The overall experience should feel warm, modern, welcoming, and highly visual.

---

# Business Goals

The website should help the business to:

* Increase daily visitors to the café.
* Encourage customers to explore the menu.
* Increase online orders.
* Promote seasonal drinks and special promotions.
* Strengthen the café's brand identity.
* Build customer trust.
* Increase Instagram and TikTok engagement.
* Make it easy for customers to find the café location.

---

# Target Audience

Primary Audience:

* Students
* Young professionals
* Freelancers
* Remote workers
* Coffee enthusiasts
* Couples
* Small communities

Age Range:

18–35 years old

---

# Brand Personality

The brand should feel:

* Warm
* Friendly
* Relaxed
* Modern
* Youthful
* Creative
* Instagram-worthy
* Authentic

Avoid:

* Corporate
* Luxury restaurant
* Old-fashioned café
* Dark industrial style
* Overly playful cartoon aesthetics

---

# Brand Positioning

Tudey Coffee is positioned as:

> "A cozy neighborhood coffee shop where people come to enjoy quality coffee, work comfortably, and create memorable moments."

The café is not trying to compete as a luxury coffee brand.

Instead, it focuses on:

* Comfortable atmosphere
* Affordable premium coffee
* Great customer experience
* Friendly service
* Beautiful interior
* Community engagement

---

# Core Message

Every page should communicate:

* Great coffee.
* Cozy atmosphere.
* Friendly people.
* Modern lifestyle.
* A place where customers want to stay longer.

# User Experience Goals

The experience should be:

* Fast
* Intuitive
* Mobile-first
* Easy to navigate
* Highly visual
* Smooth scrolling
* Comfortable to browse

Visitors should feel inspired to stay, explore, and eventually visit the café.

---

# Visual Communication

The website should rely heavily on:

* Large food photography
* Coffee close-ups
* Lifestyle imagery
* Warm colors
* Spacious layouts
* Minimal text
* Clear visual hierarchy

Images should sell the atmosphere as much as the products.

---

# Content Tone

Writing style should be:

* Friendly
* Conversational
* Relaxed
* Authentic
* Short and impactful

Avoid:

* Corporate language
* Technical jargon
* Overly promotional copy

---

# Conversion Goals

Primary conversions:

* Visit the café
* View the menu
* Contact via WhatsApp
* Open Google Maps

Secondary conversions:

* Follow Instagram
* Follow TikTok
* Share with friends
* Return for future promotions

---

# Mobile Priority

Most visitors will access the website from smartphones.

Therefore:

* Large buttons
* Fast loading
* Sticky navigation
* Easy-to-read typography
* Optimized images
* Minimal scrolling friction

---

# Success Metrics

The project will be considered successful if it:

* Increases store visits.
* Increases menu views.
* Generates more WhatsApp inquiries.
* Improves customer engagement.
* Encourages social media sharing.
* Reflects the café's warm and modern identity.

---

# Final Experience Goal

Visitors should leave the website thinking:

> "This café looks cozy, the coffee looks amazing, and I want to visit today."

The website should feel less like a digital menu and more like an extension of the café experience itself.
